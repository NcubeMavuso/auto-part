Shipping & Returns

Everything you need to know about shipping and returns

Shipping Information

Shipping Methods

Standard Shipping

5-7 business days - $9.99

Express Shipping

2-3 business days - $19.99

Free Shipping

Free on orders over $100

Shipping Policy

We ship to all 50 states and most international destinations. All orders are carefully packaged to ensure your parts arrive in perfect condition.

Processing Time

Most orders are processed within 1-2 business days. Custom or special order items may take longer.

Tracking Information

You will receive tracking information via email once your order ships. You can also track your order through our website.

International Shipping

International shipping is available to select countries. Additional customs fees and taxes may apply and are the responsibility of the customer.

Returns & Exchanges

Return Policy

We accept returns within 30 days of purchase, provided items are in original condition with all packaging and documentation.

How to Return an Item

  1. Contact us to initiate a return
  2. Receive your return authorization number
  3. Package the item securely
  4. Ship to our return address
  5. Receive your refund within 5-7 business days

Return Conditions

  • Items must be in original condition
  • All original packaging must be included
  • Installation instructions and warranties must be included
  • Custom or special order items may not be returnable

Refund Process

Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after we receive your returned item. Refunds will be issued to the original payment method.

Exchanges

We offer exchanges for defective or incorrect items. Contact us to arrange an exchange before shipping items back.

Restocking Fee

A 15% restocking fee may apply to returns that are not due to our error or defective products.

Need Help?

If you have any questions about shipping or returns, please don't hesitate to contact us. Our customer service team is here to help!
